THE BLOCKCHAIN

The Blockchain is a shared and immutable ledger organized chronologically. Information is stored in fixed-size blocks, where each row of the ledger corresponds to a block. Each block includes a growing date and time (hours, minutes, seconds), ensuring a precise timestamp of the records inside the blockchain.
